This website is about my mothers (Jocelyn) dog, Hank. His likes, dislikes, and his life.

About Hank

Hank is 4 years old and a boxer/pitbul mix. He was adoptted from a local shelter. Found on she side of the road tied to a pole with chain. When trying to break free he bit the chain, damaging all of his teeth. The shelter took him in and brought him back to health. However, he is still struglling to beat heartworms, for this he takes two doces of medication every day.

Hanks favorite things are going for walks, squeaky toys, and treats. He doesnt like loud noises and being left alone.

Jocelyn's Quote

"Everybody loves Hank! Hank has never met a person or another animal that he didnt immediatly become friends with. He is calm, laid back and gentle."
